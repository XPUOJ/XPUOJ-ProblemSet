你需要将计算结果写入 `output`，满足：

```text
A_scaled[i, k] = float(A[i, k]) * A_scale[i, floor(k / BLOCK_SIZE)]
B_scaled[j, k] = float(B[j, k]) * B_scale[floor(j / BLOCK_SIZE), floor(k / BLOCK_SIZE)]

output[i, j] = bfloat16(
    sum_k(A_scaled[i, k] * B_scaled[j, k])
)
```
