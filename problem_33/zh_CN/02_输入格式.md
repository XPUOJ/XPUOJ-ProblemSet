sectionTitle: "输入格式"
type: "Text"
---
本题的输入由评测程序在 GPU 上构造并传入 `run_kernel`。评测会生成以下参数并按顺序传入：

* `x`：bfloat16 tensor，shape `(N, C_in, H, W)`
* `weight`：bfloat16 tensor，shape `(C_out, C_in, R, S)`
* `bias`：bfloat16 tensor，shape `(C_out,)`
* `broadcast`：bfloat16 tensor，shape `(N, C_out, H_out, W_out)`
* `output`：bfloat16 tensor，shape `(N, C_out, H_out, W_out)`，作为输出缓冲区
* `N, C_in, C_out, H, W, R, S, stride, padding`：int64 标量
