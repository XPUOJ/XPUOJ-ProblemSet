sectionTitle: "输入格式"
type: "Text"
---
本题的输入由评测程序在 GPU 上构造并传入 `run_kernel`。评测会生成以下参数并按顺序传入：

* `x`：bfloat16 tensor，shape $(total\_tokens, in\_features)$，行优先连续存储
* `w1`：bfloat16 tensor，shape $(num\_groups, out\_features, in\_features)$
* `w3`：bfloat16 tensor，shape $(num\_groups, out\_features, in\_features)$
* `group_sizes`：int32 tensor，shape $(num\_groups,)$，连续存储，每组 token 数，满足 $\sum(group\_sizes) = total\_tokens$
* `out`：bfloat16 tensor，shape $(total\_tokens, out\_features \times 2)$，行优先连续存储，作为输出缓冲区，初始值未定义
* `total_tokens`：int64 标量，总 token 数
* `in_features`：int64 标量，输入特征数
* `out_features`：int64 标量，单权输出的特征数
* `num_groups`：int64 标量，组数
