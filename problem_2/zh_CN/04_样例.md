## 输入

```
A: [[1, 2, 3],
    [4, 5, 6]]        # shape (2, 3)

B: [[7, 8, 9],
    [10, 11, 12]]    # shape (2, 3)

M: 2
N: 2
K: 3
```

## 输出

```
C: [[50, 68],
    [122, 167]]      # shape (2, 2)
```

## 计算过程

本题计算 $C = A @ B^T$。首先将 $B$ 转置得到：

```
B^T: [[7, 10],
     [8, 11],
     [9, 12]]       # shape (3, 2)
```

然后计算矩阵乘法：

$C_{0,0} = 1 \times 7 + 2 \times 8 + 3 \times 9 = 7 + 16 + 27 = 50$

$C_{0,1} = 1 \times 10 + 2 \times 11 + 3 \times 12 = 10 + 22 + 36 = 68$

$C_{1,0} = 4 \times 7 + 5 \times 8 + 6 \times 9 = 28 + 40 + 54 = 122$

$C_{1,1} = 4 \times 10 + 5 \times 11 + 6 \times 12 = 40 + 55 + 72 = 167$
